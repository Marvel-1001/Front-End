The Five Friends Forever by

VIRGINIA WITTS

Synopsis

Tulonga is 11 and the leader of the group. Her nine-year- old brother, Samuel, is always up to mischief. Uno, 10, is a bookworm who has her in the clouds most of the time. Ten-year- old Nico is Mr. Fix it. He is also kind and gentle, but sometimes he hides his softer side by being too macho. Nature-lover Kayla is nine but thinks she is already 11. She can't sit still and is a creative and extrovert.
